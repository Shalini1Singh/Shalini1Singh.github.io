ABOUT
Hi, I am SHALINI SINGH, a Textile Design student from the National Institute of Fashion Technology Kolkata.

I am a radiant, happy, and playful girl who always occupies herself with some or other work.

I might be a person of few words, yet on the contrary, when you see my work, you might hear its peak volume.

My design aesthetics is minimal, textural, bold, asymmetrical, blending and synchronizing in a subtle color palette.

I understand design as a reflection of my personality, a little that would suffice and balance.
